---
title: Payment Methods
description: Checkout behavior for Credit Card, ACH, and Text‑to‑Pay.
---

## Credit Card
- Secure hosted fields; tokenized in-browser
- Provider auto-selects based on your AuxPay config
- Title shows “(Test Mode)” when enabled

## ACH
- Simple form: Account Number + Routing Number
- Validated and sent securely on order placement
- Title shows “(Test Mode)” when enabled

## Text‑to‑Pay
- No fields at checkout
- After placing the order, an SMS link is sent to the customer
- Order status auto-updates after payment via webhook

<Callout>
All methods work with both Classic and Blocks checkout.
</Callout>
